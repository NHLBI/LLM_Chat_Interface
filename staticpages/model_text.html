Active Models in NHLBI Chat

GPT-4o

This is the most advanced multimodal model (images and text). The model has 128K context and an October 2023 knowledge cutoff. This model is great for your everyday needs.

o1

o1 is the new reasoning model series for complex tasks, tailored for coding, math, and science use cases. The model has 200K context and an October 2023 knowledge cutoff. o1 is well-suited for complex workloads where maintaining an extended narrative or detailed information is critical.

o3-mini

Much like o1, the o3-mini model is designed for high accuracy and robust performance, with training data that extends to December 2024 and a 100,000 token context window. Its extended training data and large context capacity make o3-mini an excellent choice for applications that benefit from the latest available information and require a broad scope of contextual memory.

DALL·E 3

The DALL·E 3 model creates images based on textual prompts. Unlike the text based models, DALL-E3 will only use your most recent user prompt to generate an image. It does not have knowledge of previous images or prompts. The best images result from very descriptive user prompts.
